Interim Government of Bangladesh

An interim government led by the Yunus ministry in Bangladesh was formed on 8 August 2024,following the resignation of the Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina 3 days prior in the face of the students protests against the government.The Supreme Court of Bangladesh affrimed the legality of the government on 9 August 2024.President Mohammed Shahabuddin administered the oath of office to Yunus and his council of advisers at Bangabhaban on 8 August 2024.
